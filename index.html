LondonIndependent studio · Est. 2017

A studio, not
an agency.

Small on purpose. The team you meet is the team that does the work.

Built on trust, driven by results.

We started Sorted Solutions in 2017 because we’d worked at big agencies and small ones and knew what was broken about both. Big shops had process but not care. Small shops had care but not the bench to ship hard things.

So we built something in the middle. Independent, partner-owned, deliberately under twenty people. Engineers who can design. Designers who can edit video. Senior people who stay on your project — not juniors running a pitch deck.

Today we lead with what we’re best at — Web Development, Social Media Marketing and SEO — and back it up with AI automation, software, creative production, and the infrastructure underneath. Seven years in, our oldest clients are still our clients. That’s the metric we care about.

Pretty is downstream of functional. We don't ship something that looks great and crashes. Order matters.

Documents over conversations.

Boring tech, mostly.

We use the right tool, not the fashionable one. Boring scales. Postgres usually beats fancy.

Transparent pricing.

Fixed-scope where possible. No "while we're in there" surprises. If we underestimate, we eat it.

Long relationships.

We'd rather have ten clients for five years than fifty for six months. Our economics support staying.
